# AdSync Brand Voice Reference

This document contains the complete vocabulary and tone guidelines for all AdSync marketing communications.

## Vocabulary Rules

### Payment & Billing

| ✅ Use This | ❌ Not This | Why |
|------------|------------|-----|
| Prepaid Access | Recurring Billing | "Prepaid" = control; "Recurring" = trap |
| Membership | Subscription | Feels exclusive vs. obligatory |
| Naira (₦) | Dollars ($) | Emphasize local currency |
| Paystack | International Gateway | Builds trust with familiar brand |
| Bank Transfer | Wire Transfer | Local terminology |
| Monthly Access | Monthly Charge | Positive framing |

### Product & Features

| ✅ Use This | ❌ Not This | Why |
|------------|------------|-----|
| AI Studio | Image Editor | Emphasizes intelligence |
| AI Consultant | Chatbot / Bot | Builds trust, humanizes |
| Creative Assistant | Template Tool | Emphasizes AI capability |
| Campaign Builder | Ad Manager | Less intimidating |
| WhatsApp Link | Landing Page | Most users lack websites |

### Audience & Market

| ✅ Use This | ❌ Not This | Why |
|------------|------------|-----|

| SME / Small Business | Enterprise / Startup | Accurate market segment |

### Competitors

## Tone Spectrum

AdSync's voice adapts to context while maintaining core principles:

### Professional (Settings, Billing, Legal)
- **Style:** Clear, direct, no fluff
- **Example:** "Your payment of ₦15,000 has been confirmed. Access renewed until March 15."
- **When:** Settings pages, receipts, terms of service

### Empathetic (Onboarding, Support, Error States)
- **Style:** Understanding, helpful, not condescending
- **Example:** "Card declined? No stress—that's common with international tools. Try bank transfer instead."
- **When:** Payment failures, first-time setup, help docs

### Motivational (Landing Page, Social Media)
- **Style:** Energizing, aspirational, locally grounded
- **Example:** "Your competitors launched 3 campaigns this week. Start yours in 2 minutes."
- **When:** Hero sections, CTA buttons, social posts

### Educational (Blog, Tutorials)
- **Style:** Expert but accessible, teaching not preaching
- **Example:** "Meta charges per impression, not per click. Here's why that matters for your budget..."
- **When:** Blog posts, knowledge base, tooltips

## Cultural Nuance

### What to Embrace
- **Reality of Cash Economy:** "Pay via transfer" not just "Pay with card"
- **WhatsApp Primacy:** "Most customers are on WhatsApp" not "Consider WhatsApp"
- **Phone-First World:** "Take photos with your phone" not "Upload high-res images"
- **Hustle Mentality:** "Launch faster than your competition" not "Enjoy the journey"

### What to Avoid
- **Excessive Pidgin:** Keep professional. Reserve for specific creative campaigns only.
- **Poverty Framing:** Never imply "you can't afford better." Position as "you deserve better."
- **Unrealistic Promises:** No "Get rich quick" or "Guaranteed sales."
- **Western Assumptions:** Don't assume credit cards, websites, or desktop computers.

## Emotional Positioning

### Primary Emotions to Evoke
1. **Relief:** "Finally, a tool that works for me"
2. **Empowerment:** "I can do this myself"
3. **Pride:** "My ads look professional"
4. **Control:** "I own the process"

### Emotions to Avoid
1. **Desperation:** Never frame as "last hope"
2. **Confusion:** Every message should be crystal clear
3. **Inadequacy:** Don't make users feel stupid for not knowing
4. **FOMO:** Light urgency okay, but no aggressive scarcity

## Writing Patterns

### Sentence Structure
- **Short is strong:** Average 10-15 words per sentence
- **Active voice:** "AdSync builds your campaign" not "Your campaign is built"
- **Second person:** "You launch in minutes" not "Users launch in minutes"

### Paragraph Structure
- **Max 3 sentences:** Anything longer needs a break
- **One idea per paragraph:** Don't mix concepts
- **Lead with benefit:** Not feature-first

### CTA Guidelines
- **Specific action:** "Start Free Trial" not "Learn More"
- **Outcome-oriented:** "Launch Your First Campaign" not "Sign Up"
- **Time-bounded:** "Create Your Ad in 2 Minutes" not "Create Your Ad"

## Examples by Context

### Payment Success Message
```
✅ "Payment confirmed! Your AdSync membership is active. Launch your first campaign now."
❌ "Transaction successful. Subscription renewed. Return to dashboard."
```

### Error State
```
✅ "Oops! Meta's API is slow right now. Your campaign saved—we'll retry in 2 minutes."
❌ "Error 500: API timeout. Please try again later."
```

### Feature Announcement
```
✅ "New: AI Studio now turns your product photos into lifestyle scenes. Try it free."
❌ "We've released AI Studio with background generation capabilities."
```

### Competitor Comparison
```
✅ "Unlike Wask, AdSync works with your Nigerian bank card. No international payment hassles."
❌ "Wask sucks. We're better. Join us."
```

## Localization Notes

### Currency Display
- Always show: ₦15,000 (with comma separator)
- Never show: NGN 15000 or N15000

### Date Format
- Use: 15 March 2026 (day month year)
- Avoid: 03/15/26 (ambiguous)

### Time Format
- Use: 2:30 PM WAT (West Africa Time)
- Context: Most users in Lagos (GMT+1)

### Phone Numbers
- Format: +234 803 123 4567
- Note: Most users expect WhatsApp contact

## Voice Checklist

Before publishing any copy, verify:
- [ ] Uses approved vocabulary from tables above
- [ ] Tone matches context (professional/empathetic/motivational)
- [ ] Acknowledges local realities (phone-first, WhatsApp, Naira)
- [ ] Clear and actionable (no jargon or ambiguity)
- [ ] Emotionally appropriate (relief, empowerment, not desperation)
- [ ] Culturally sensitive (no Western assumptions)
- [ ] Brand-consistent (AdSync = reliable fintech vibe)
