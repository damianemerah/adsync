# Product Requirements Document: AdSync

**Version:** 1.1 (Growth & refinement Phase)
**Status:** Active Development
**Target Market:** Nigerian & Pan-African SMEs (Mobile-first)

---

## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Product Vision

AdSync is the "operating system" for African SME marketing. It democratizes ad access by stripping away the complexity of global tools (Meta Ads Manager) and replacing it with an **Africa-first, mobile-native experience** that handles local payments, creative generation, and strategy.

### 1.2 The Core Problem

1.  **Complexity:** Global tools assume desktop usage and marketing degrees.
2.  **Payment Friction:** International USD payments fail for 60% of African merchants.
3.  **Trust Deficit:** Existing tools (Wask, etc.) are perceived as "scams" due to hard cancellations and opaque billing.
4.  **Creative Gap:** Users lack skills to design ads that convert in the local context.

### 1.3 The Solution

- **Prepaid "Naira-Native" Billing:** Pay via Paystack/Transfer. No recurring USD traps.
- **AdSync Studio:** AI-powered creative engine (Flux.1) for local visual context.
- **Consultant AI:** "GPT-4o-mini" strategist for targeting and copy.
- **Transparent UX:** Live Previews, Interactive JSON targeting, and "Why" analytics.
- **Free Growth Tools:** "African Ad Scorecard" to build trust before sign-up.

## 3. User Personas

### 3.1 "Vendor Veronica" (The Social Seller)

- **Goal:** "I posted a bag on IG, I want to sell 10 today."
- **Needs:** Mobile-first wizard, simplified targeting ("People who like Fashion"), WhatsApp CTA.
- **Friction:** Scared of "wasting money" on ads that don't work.

### 3.2 "Agency Ahmed" (The Local Freelancer)

- **Goal:** Manage 10 clients without logging in/out.
- **Needs:** Reporting dashboard to prove value, "Collaborative Mode" for approvals.
- **Friction:** Clients don't trust him with their cards (AdSync Prepaid solves this).

## 4. Functional Requirements

### 4.1 Authentication & Org

- **FR-01:** Multi-tenancy (Organizations).
- **FR-02:** Email/Google Auth.
- **FR-03:** "Agency Mode" (Switch between Orgs easily).

### 4.2 The "Non-Linear" Campaign Wizard (UX Upgrade)

- **FR-04: Collapsible Step Tabs:** Users can jump between Goal <-> Audience <-> Creative <-> Launch.
- **FR-05: Live Ad Preview:** A sticky right-sidebar showing the _actual_ ad mockup (TikTok/FB Feed) updating in real-time.
- **FR-06: Smart Skip:** If a user has a winning post, skip "Creative" step.
- **FR-07: ROAS Predictor:** Real-time badge showing "Estimated Reach" and "Predicted ROAS" based on benchmarks.

### 4.3 AI & Intelligence

- **FR-08: Transparent AI:** Show the "Raw JSON" targeting generated by AI, allow power users to edit it.
- **FR-09: One-Click Remix:** "This ad worked. Generate 3 variations" (Change background, rewrite headline).
- **FR-10: Fatigue Detection:** Alert users when a winning ad's CTR drops below 1%.

### 4.4 Creative Engine

- **FR-11: Africa-First Templates:** Pre-loaded templates for local niches (e.g., "Jumia Black Friday", "Detty December", "Ramadan Sales").
- **FR-12: AI Vision Analysis:** Analyze _why_ an ad is winning ("Bright red background works best for your shoe ads").

### 4.5 Billing & Trust

- **FR-13: Prepaid Access:** 30-day passes. No auto-renewal.
- **FR-14: Free Tools (Top of Funnel):**
  - **"Ad Scorecard":** Public page where users paste a Link, and we grade their ad setup (Free lead mag).
  - **"Competitor Spy":** See what ads are running in a specific Nigerian niche.

---

## 5. Technical Architecture

- **Frontend:** Next.js 16 (PWA enabled for offline caching).
- **State:** Zustand (Client) + TanStack Query (Server).
- **AI:** OpenAI (Logic), Fal.ai (Images), Kling/Veo (Video - Roadmap).
- **Database:** Supabase (RLS enabled).
- **Payments:** Paystack (Webhooks required for strictly verifying payments).

### Phase 3: Growth & "Viral" Tools

- [ ] **Free Ad Scorecard:** The "Wask Killer" lead magnet.
- [ ] **WhatsApp Quick Actions:** "Pause Ad" via WhatsApp command.
- [ ] **Agency Collaboration:** Commenting on creatives.
- [ ] **TikTok Integration.**
- [ ] **Self-Driving Sub-Placement Optimization:** Auto-pause underperforming surfaces (e.g., Feed) and shift budget to winners (e.g., Reels) without manual intervention.
- [ ] **Pro Split Testing:** Automatically split overlapping placements (like Feed vs Stories) into separate Ad Sets to force Meta algorithms to spend budget evenly and find true winners.
