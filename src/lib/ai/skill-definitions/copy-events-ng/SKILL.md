# Nigerian Events Copy

## Skeleton
LINE 1 HOOK (occasion urgency or outcome) → LINES 2-3 WHAT YOU HANDLE (delegation relief) → LINE 4 PROOF → LINE 5 CTA

## Hook Patterns

| Occasion       | Hook angle                                                                     |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| weddings       | "Your wedding day, sorted — from aso-ebi to last dance"                        |
| introductions  | "Introduction ceremony done properly — décor, fabric, coordination"            |
| birthday       | "A birthday they'll talk about for years. We make that happen."                |
| corporate      | "Your brand deserves an event that represents it properly."                    |
| decoration     | "Transform any space — from bare hall to magazine-worthy venue"                |
| aso-ebi        | "The aso-ebi your guests will actually want to wear"                           |
| general        | "Every event needs one person who handles everything. That's us."              |

## Benefit Lines — Delegation Relief + Specificity
State exactly what you handle. Buyers are overwhelmed — "you just show up and enjoy" is the strongest promise in this vertical.

- Full service: "Décor, catering coordination, MC, security, cleanup — you show up and enjoy your day"
- Capacity: "10 to 500 guests — we've handled both ends"
- Location range: "We've worked across Lagos — Eko Hotel, Oriental, private villas in Lekki"
- Timeline commitment: "Book 4+ weeks out — we send detailed timeline and confirm everything 2 weeks before"
- Aso-ebi: "Fabric quality, price per yard, minimum order, colors — all in one package. Nationwide distribution available."

## Proof
✅ "Booked every weekend this season" / "Clients come back for every family event" / "We've coordinated [X] events this year" (if stated)
✅ Repeat client behavior is the strongest signal in events — it implies reliability across multiple high-stakes occasions
❌ Never: fabricated event counts, "5-star rated" without data, "Lagos's best event planner"

## CTA
- WhatsApp: "WhatsApp us your event date, size, and venue — we send a full proposal same day"
- Booking: "Share your date — we check availability and confirm within the hour"
- Learn more: "Tap to see our event portfolio and get in touch."

## WhatsApp Prefill
- wedding/intro: "Hi! [Wedding/Introduction] in [loc] on [date], ~[X] guests. Décor/coordination packages and pricing?"
- birthday: "Hi! Birthday event for ~[X] people on [date] in [loc]. What packages do you offer?"
- aso-ebi: "Hi! Aso-ebi for [event type] in [loc]. Fabric options, price per yard, and minimum order?"
- decorator: "Hi! Need decoration for [event] on [date] at [venue]. Can you share your packages?"

## Headlines (≤40 chars)
- Occasion: "Your Lagos Event, Professionally Done" | "Aso-Ebi Every Guest Will Love"
- Urgency: "Book Your Date — Weekends Fill Fast" | "Only 3 [Month] Slots Remaining"

## Hook Taxonomy for Events

Use different hook types across copy variations:
| Hook Type   | Events Example                                                                    |
| ----------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Story**       | "Last Saturday, a bride told us our décor made her guests forget about the food." |
| **Problem**     | "Tired of event vendors who confirm, then go quiet two days before your event?"   |
| **Curiosity**   | "I was wrong about DIY event décor. Here's what I learned the hard way."         |
| **Value**       | "How to host 200 guests in Lagos without a single vendor crisis on the day"       |
| **JTBD**        | "Not hiring a decorator — hiring the confidence that your venue will wow them"    |
| **Urgency**     | "Only 2 Saturday slots left in [month]. One might be yours."                     |

**Meta 125-char rule**: Count your hook. Events ads need the date/occasion signal early.
Example: "Your wedding day, sorted. From aso-ebi to last dance. Lagos events team." = 73 chars ✅

## Deadline Urgency (events-specific)
Events have natural deadlines — use them. This is the only vertical where real urgency is always available:
- "Your [event] is [X] weeks away" → factual urgency, not fabricated scarcity
- "Weekend dates fill by [Month]" → season-specific demand
- "Book 4 weeks out for full coordination" → positions early booking as a quality signal, not just urgency

## Aso-Ebi Specific Rules
- Always mention: fabric quality + price per yard or set + minimum order + available colors
- Delivery scope: nationwide distribution if available (many Lagos aso-ebi sellers ship nationwide)
- Payment: "Pay per yard / full set / deposit to reserve your colour"
- WhatsApp-first CTA for aso-ebi is almost always correct — buyers always want to see fabric photos first

## Psychology Layer
- **Deadline as organic urgency**: Unlike other verticals, events always have a real date. "Your wedding is in 6 weeks — we still have your date" is factual urgency that doesn't feel pushy.
- **Delegation relief is the primary hook**: Event buyers are stressed. "We handle everything — you enjoy your day" is more powerful than listing services. Lead with the feeling of relief, then back it up with specifics.
- **Portfolio as trust signal**: In events, images sell. CTA should drive to portfolio/DM to see photos. "See our work" > "Contact us".
- **Repeat clients as proof**: "Clients come back for every family event" implies consistent excellence across high-stakes occasions — stronger than any testimonial.
- **AB variation angles**: Var A = outcome ("A day they'll talk about for years"), Var B = relief ("We handle everything — you just show up and enjoy"), Var C = deadline urgency ("Only 2 [month] weekend dates left — is yours available?")

❌ Never: "magical memories", "once-in-a-lifetime experience", "unforgettable moments", "world-class events", "stress-free planning"
