# Nigerian Fashion Copy

## Skeleton (output labels NEVER appear in final copy)

LINE 1 HOOK (≤12 words) → LINES 2-3 BENEFIT → LINE 4 PROOF (mandatory) → LINE 5 CTA

## Hook by tier

| Tier        | Pattern                        | Examples                                                                                  |
| ----------- | ------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| low ₦1-10k  | Lead with price/access         | "Lagos fashion from ₦3,500 only 👗" / "Quality bags from ₦5k. Yes, really."               |
| mid ₦10-40k | Lead with desire/social proof  | "The gown Lagos ladies keep coming back for" / "This style sold out twice — we restocked" |
| high ₦40k+  | Lead with exclusivity/scarcity | "Only 6 pieces available." / "Handpicked for the woman who knows what she wants"          |

## Benefit lines — pick ONE differentiator

clothing/gowns: event fit | fabric quality | custom sizing | style range
bags: durability | size/versatility | genuine leather
wigs: "100% human hair — no shedding, no wahala" | longevity | pre-styled

- Always add location line: "Lagos-based, ships nationwide" / "Same-day delivery within Abuja"

## Proof (mandatory — no fabrication)

✅ Use if stated: exact numbers/ratings
✅ If no proof given: "Trusted by Lagos ladies from Island to Mainland" / "Keeps selling out — restock by demand" / "Our customers keep coming back"
❌ Never: fabricated orders/ratings/superlatives

## CTA

WhatsApp: "Send us a message — full collection and prices in minutes" / "DM us. We reply fast."
Buy now: "Tap to shop — delivery nationwide in 2-3 days"

## WhatsApp prefill

"Hi! I saw your ad for [product] in [location]. What styles/sizes do you have, how much, and do you deliver?"

## Headlines (≤40 chars, 1 benefit + 1 curiosity/urgency)

Benefit: "Lagos Fashion, Prices You'll Love" | "Quality Ankara. Fast Delivery"
Urgency: "This Style Keeps Selling Out" | "Only 6 Pieces Left — Don't Wait"

❌ Never: "elevate your wardrobe","experience fashion differently","unleash your inner fashionista"
