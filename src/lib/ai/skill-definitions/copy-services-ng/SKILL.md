## Universal Hook Taxonomy

Before picking a hook pattern, choose the motivational angle:

| Hook Type      | Formula                             | Nigerian Example                                                      |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Curiosity**  | "I was wrong about [X] until..."    | "I was wrong about outsourcing. Then we cut costs by 40%."            |
| **Story**      | "Last [week/month], [someone]..."   | "Last month, a Lagos startup saved 6 hours a week using our tool."    |
| **Value**      | "How to [outcome] without [cost]"   | "How to manage deliveries without chasing drivers all day"            |
| **Contrarian** | "Unpopular opinion: [true thing]"   | "Unpopular opinion: most Lagos service providers just don't show up." |
| **Urgency**    | "[Number] [people] already [did X]" | "47 Lagos businesses switched this month — here's why."               |
| **Problem**    | "[Pain]? [Empathy statement]."      | "Tired of [service providers] who ghost you? We get it."              |

**Use different hook types across variations** — never write two variations with the same hook type.

### JTBD Framing

People don't buy services. They hire them to do a job.

| Service       | What they're actually hiring it for                                   |
| ------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Event planner | "Have my guests impressed the moment they walk in — with zero stress" |
| Logistics     | "Ship orders across Lagos without chasing drivers or losing packages" |
| Agency/B2B    | "Grow the business without adding 3 more people to payroll"           |

Frame at least ONE copy variation around the job, not the service.

## SERVICES & B2B

_Applies to: salon, photographer, event planner, decorator, logistics, repair, clinic, tutor, consultant, agency, cleaning, laundry, mechanic, B2B, general_

### Skeleton

LINE 1 HOOK (outcome/problem solved) → LINES 2-3 BENEFIT (reliability + specificity) → LINE 4 PROOF → LINE 5 CTA (sets expectation)

### Hook Patterns

| Service          | Hook angle                                                    |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| salon/stylist    | "Get your hair done by a stylist Lagos keeps rebooking"       |
| photographer     | "Photos that tell your story the way you want it told"        |
| event planner    | "Your event, handled. From setup to takedown."                |
| logistics        | "Same-day delivery across Lagos — tracked, reliable, on time" |
| repair/tradesman | "AC not cooling? We fix it today."                            |
| tutoring         | "Your child's grades improve or we work until they do"        |
| agency/B2B       | "We handle your [service] so you can handle your business"    |

### Benefit Lines — reliability + specificity always

- trust: "We confirm every booking. We show up. We deliver." / "Same-day response — no ghosting"
- coverage: "We serve Lekki, VI, Surulere, Yaba, Ikeja and most of Lagos"
- expertise: "Trained in [technique]" / "2 backup cameras — no equipment failure on your day"
- turnaround: "Photos in 48hrs" / "Repairs same-day for most faults" / "Quote in 10 mins on WhatsApp"

### Proof

✅ "Trusted by Lagos clients across events, businesses, homes" / "Repeat bookings are our biggest referral source" / "Schedule fills weeks ahead — book early"
❌ Never: "Lagos's #1 [service]", "100% success rate", "world-class"

### CTA

- WhatsApp: "WhatsApp us — quote same day" / "Send your date and details — confirm availability immediately"
- Learn more: "Tap to see our full list of services and learn more."

### WhatsApp Prefill by Type

- salon: "Hi! [service] in [loc]. Availability this week and price for [specific]?"
- photographer: "Hi! Need photographer for [event type] on [date] in [loc]. Packages and pricing?"
- logistics: "Hi! Moving [item] from [area A] to [area B]. Cost and how fast?"
- repair: "Hi! [fault] at my [home/office] in [loc]. When can you come?"
- B2B: "Hi! We need [service] for our business. Can you share pricing and availability?"

### Headlines (≤40 chars)

- Benefit: "Lagos Services That Actually Show Up" | "Same-Day Delivery Across Lagos"
- Urgency: "Why Lagos Clients Keep Rebooking Us" | "Weekend Slots Filling Fast"

### Psychology Layer

- **Reliability as the primary hook (Lagos context)**: In Lagos, trust is the real purchase driver. Lead with evidence of showing up: "We confirm. We show up. We deliver."
- **Specificity makes claims real**: "Response in 10 mins on WhatsApp" > "fast response".
- **Retention as social proof**: "Repeat bookings are our biggest referral source" implies consistent results.
- **AB variation angles**: Var A = problem-led ("Tired of [service providers] who ghost you?"), Var B = outcome ("Your project/event/home, handled"), Var C = speed ("WhatsApp us. Quote in 10 minutes.")

❌ Never: "elevate your experience", "world-class service", "passion-driven team", "one-stop shop"
