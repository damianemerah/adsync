# Nigerian Food Copy

## Skeleton

LINE 1 HOOK (sensory/occasion) → LINES 2-3 BENEFIT → LINE 4 PROOF → LINE 5 CTA

## Hook patterns

sensory: "The shawarma Lekki keeps ordering 🔥" / "Jollof that smells like home. Tastes better." / "This pepper soup hits different on a rainy day"
occasion: "Planning an event in Lagos? Your food problem is solved." / "Birthday, burial, corporate — we cater for all"
delivery: "Order now. Hot food at your door in [X] mins." / "Hungry in Lagos? We're already on the way."
price: "A full plate of jollof and chicken for ₦2,500"

## Benefit lines

freshness: "Made fresh daily — no reheated, no yesterday's batch" / "Cook-to-order. You taste the difference."
delivery: "Hot delivery across Lagos — Lekki, VI, Surulere, Yaba. Order by 12pm for same-day."
caterers: "10 to 1,000 people — birthday, corporate, burial, wedding. Full setup included."
cakes: "Custom flavour, custom design — baked on order day. No frozen."
local food: "Homestyle Nigerian cooking — amala, egusi, stew. The real thing."

## Proof

✅ "Lagos customers keep reordering every weekend" / "Popular for corporate lunches and events" / "Fully booked most weekends — order early"
❌ Never: "Nigeria's best shawarma","over 1,000 orders" (unless stated)

## CTA

WhatsApp: "WhatsApp us to place your order — confirmed in under 5 minutes" / "DM for full menu, pricing, delivery areas"
Catering: "Send us your event date and guest count — we quote same day"

## WhatsApp prefill

delivery: "Hi! I'd like to order [food]. Menu, prices, delivery to [area]?"
caterer: "Hi! Event for ~[X] people on [date]. Can you send menu and pricing?"
cake: "Hi! Custom cake for [occasion]. Flavours and price for [size]?"

## Headlines

Benefit: "Fresh Lagos Food Delivered to You" | "Catering That Makes Events Memorable"
Urgency: "Book Before This Weekend Fills Up" | "Why Lagos Keeps Ordering This"
